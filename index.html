Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Alejandro Blanco ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Digital Chief of Staff automated messaging service. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information related to our SMS and voice communication services.

Information We Collect

We collect only the phone numbers and names of individuals who have provided verbal or written consent to receive messages. We may also collect message history for the purpose of managing appointments, reminders, and follow-ups.

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers are used solely for sending appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-up messages, and other communications initiated by or on behalf of Alejandro Blanco. We do not use your information for marketing to third parties.

No Sharing of Mobile Numbers

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your mobile phone number to any third parties for their marketing purposes. Your number is used only for direct communication from Alejandro Blanco's Digital Chief of Staff service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions and appointments. You may receive between 1 and 20 messages per month depending on scheduled activities and follow-ups.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply. Please contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opting Out

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us directly to request removal from our messaging list.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the information we collect. Communication data is stored on secure, access-controlled servers.
